GAWI BAWI BO

SPRING 2019

CLIENT BACKGROUND

Gawi Bawi Bo is a potential restaurant group which currently owns Hinode Japanese Steakhouse (Springfield and Nixa) and Bawi Korean BBQ.

OBJECTIVE

Research and develop a company analysis on the two branches to see where both branches are currently positioned in the market, their strengths, and what could be improved moving forward. This included a SWOT analysis, Target Market Analysis, and Financial Analysis. I then gave general recommendations to the company.
