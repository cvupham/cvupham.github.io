GREEK YOGURT DOG FOOD

FALL 2018

CLIENT BACKGROUND

This was a made up client who started her own Greek yogurt dog food. The family history goes like this: Years ago, her great-great-great grandmother saved the family dog by spoon feeding it Greek yogurt. The dog had gotten very ill and, when taken to the vet, was told that it was fatal. Her great-great-great grandmother did not accept that and took it into her own hands to save the dog. Greek yogurt has many healing properties, especially for gut health. That was how the client got the idea to start her own dog food.

OBJECTIVE

The client had no branding whatsover. She only had the product itself. The dog food needed a name, package design, and a logo. We were asked to come up with a three-series ad campaign that will introduce the product to the market.
